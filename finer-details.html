Questions, Answered

Can we bring the kids?

Yes! Children are welcome — this is a family celebration. Just include everyone in your party count when you RSVP so we can plan seats and plates.

When should I RSVP by?

Please respond by November 8, 2026. The RSVP form takes about thirty seconds.

What time should I arrive?

Plan to arrive by 3:30 PM. The ceremony begins promptly at 4:00 PM.

Can I take photos during the ceremony?

We’re having an unplugged ceremony — we’d love to see your faces, not your phones, while we say our vows. Our photographer will capture everything. Once the reception starts, snap away and tag #pushingP!

What exactly does black tie mean?

For him: a tuxedo or a dark, formal suit with a bow tie or formal necktie. For her: a floor-length gown or equally elegant formal evening wear. When in doubt, dress up rather than down — you won’t be overdressed.

Should I bring a gift to the wedding?

Your presence is truly the present. If you’d like to give, our honeymoon fund and registry can be found online — no need to carry anything to the venue.

Where should I stay?

We have a hotel block at Margaritaville Lake Conroe — details just below.
